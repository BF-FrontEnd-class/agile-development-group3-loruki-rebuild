Introduction

A hosting platform is a service that provides individuals and businesses with the ability to publish their website on the internet. These platforms allow website owners to store their website files on servers that are connected to the internet, making their website accessible to visitors around the world. Hosting platforms offer a variety of features and tools to help website owners manage their websites, such as control panels for website administration, website builders for creating and designing websites, and customer support for technical assistance.
